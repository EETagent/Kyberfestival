Petr Pavel - Spolu silnější

RETIRED ARMY GENERAL OF THE ARMY OF THE CZECH REPUBLIC

Retired Army General, former chief of the General Staff of the Army of the Czech Republic and chairman of the NATO Military Committee. He received many Czech and international military honors for his services. Today he gives lectures and discussions with citizens. In the spring of 2020, he founded the Spolu silnější initiative, first in the form of a collection that helps people on the front lines to fight the epidemic and those most affected by the crisis. He later put together with experts recommendations for better crisis management and now strives a thorough review of crisis management of our country. He endeavor to lead by example in active citizenship and volunteering.
